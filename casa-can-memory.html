Description

Casa Can Memory is located in a quiet area of San Eulalia, right on the seafront.

The bright, pretty, air-conditioned rooms at Casa Can Memory Ibiza feature a furnished terrace with side or full views of the Mediterranean Sea. They also include a private bathroom.

8 must-know tips for a successful hotel renovation or refurbishment

Ask us or ask anyone who has done it, renovating or refurbishing a villa isn’t easy. But, it is also unavoidable to keep your property in top shape and selling well. Here are some handy tips to keep your property’s makeover hassle-free.

Your busiest time is really not when you should be pulling down walls or changing curtains. Review the property’s occupancy trends of the prior years to determine its off-season—this is the ideal window for the renovation. Your channel manager and booking engine insights are a good place to get this information.

Make sure you allow a buffer at the end of the project. Constructions and refurbishments hardly ever go as per plan. It’s also a good idea to let everyone know your deadline at the start of the project.

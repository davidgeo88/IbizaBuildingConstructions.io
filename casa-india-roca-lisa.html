Casa India, Roca Lisa

Presentation

This Luxury villa located in the exclusive neighbourhood of Roca Lisa. With incredible outdoor space overlooking the countryside, beautifully redesigned interiors and six bedrooms, this modern propriety got 5000m2 .

The villa also features:.

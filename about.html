Over 17 Years of Construction Experience

Founded in 2003, Ibiza Building Constructions has developed an extensive knowledge base of construction project experience spanning various conditions and environments. We have successfully completed projects in settings ranging from healthcare, where the site must be kept clean, quiet, and safe, to major highway interchanges where commuter traffic presents an uncontrollable variable that must be constantly managed.

An Industry Leader

Ibiza Building Constructions has consistently ranked as one of the country’s top 400 construction companies for 10 years and we are one of the largest construction companies from Ibiza Island. Our clients include some of the most well-respected brands in the region and throughout the world. Through our industry leadership, we are frequently asked to provide input into industry legislation and participate in industry roundtables.

A Company with Vast Capabilities

From building and road construction projects to mason products and construction materials supply, Ibiza Building Constructions has a vast set of capabilities and extensive experience that allow us to successfully complete complex, challenging, and unique projects on time and within budget.

An Extensive Equipment Fleet

Recognized by industry publications and leading equipment manufacturers like Caterpillar, we have one of the most comprehensive and well-maintained company-owned equipment inventories in the industry. Our fleet includes over 200 pieces of equipment. We operate six repair facilities that provide complete, in-house repair capabilities for equipment ranging from cranes and concrete mixers to small power equipment.

We are here to cover all of your property needs with a high-quality service that comes at an affordable cost. We offer an extensive range of services to both homes and businesses, using the most innovative technology on the market.

Electric Services

  • Electrical Repairs

    We can undertake minor works such as repairs, installation of new sockets and switches. It gives peace of mind to have a qualified electrician carry out the works. You can choose to have repairs done when needed, or we can provide maintenance contracts to commercial clients.

  • Appliance Installation

    Installing new appliances doesn’t need to be a complicated process. Here at Ibiza Building Constructions, we have vast experience in the installation of both residential appliances like kitchen ovens and hobs to commercial appliances such as fridge freezers.

    This type of job involves the removal and installation of new appliances, as well as the rewiring that might have to go with it.

  • Lighting Installation

    Our residential and commercial electricians can undertake any lighting work. You may have a new lighting system that you may want to be installed inside or outside of your home. Commercial spaces may also need the installation of security lighting, which again is something our electricians can do. No job is too big or too small, just know our helpful and friendly staff will put your mind at ease.

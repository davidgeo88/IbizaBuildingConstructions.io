Description

Villa has a capacity for 6 Guests.

Charming minimalist villa located in the north of Ibiza, close to San Miguel, about 30 min from Ibiza town. Large terraces and pool which melts with the blue colour of the sea.

The house and interior design are made by the architect Ramón Esteve and consist of: Entrance level:

1 dining room

1 well equipped kitchen

2 bedrooms each one with 1 bed size 180cm

Level +1:

1 bedroom with 1 bed size 180cm, private bathroom and dressing

Our project

Our project with this villa consists of building from the beginning to the end. The simple, modern interior materials and composition are inspired by the agrarian landscape and are enhanced by unique lighting elements.
